hn-comment-display-modifier

Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmark bar. When looking at news.ycombinator.com/item?id=something, click the bookmarklet to hide all comments that are not at the top level.

A grey line will appear above the user and time of a comment, if the comment has replies. Click the comment body to reveal all direct replies.
